Signing up

  1. Click on the signup button and select a caetgory. There are users, landlord and agent category
  2. Only Landlords and agents are given the right to upload apartments.
  3. Fill in all necessary details and click on the submit button
  4. Complete your account creation by verifying and activating your email.
  5. Once actiavted, your email will be displayed at the navigation menu. You can click on your email to access your dashboard

Getting an Apartment

Once signed in, you will be redirected to the apartment page in order for you to scroll through our available apartments. Pick an apartment of your choice and pay the required fee using our integrated payment gateway. You can also pay from your wallet if your wallet have equal or greater the apartment price.

Money Back Gaurantee Policy

We are curious about offering the best on our platform. We have a money back gaurantee policy that gives you back the total money you paid for an apartment once you don`t like the apartment.

Once you have secured an apartment, the agent/landlord who uploaded such apartment will be notified and the money you paid will be moved into your transaction wallet. You can`t withdraw available funds from your transaction wallet except from funds present in your main wallet. you are given a maximum of 3days to make contact with the agent/landlord in charge of that apartment.

Within 3days, you are also required to visit the location of your apartment and check the apartment if you love the apartment. On your dashboard, under the "My Apartment" tab, you can select more details and activate the apartment. The apartment verification has 3 unique buttons;

  1. I Love this apartment: On click of this button, the amount of the apartment paid is moved from your transaction wallet to the agent/landlord wallet. And the apartment status is changed from "pending" to "rented". Kindly note that after a year, you will be reqiured to make another payment for such apartment. or you could quit if you don`t want to stay there anymore.
  2. I hate this apartment: On click of this button, the amount of the apartment paid is moved from your transaction wallet to your main wallet. You can either withdraw the money available in your main wallet or use it to get another apartment. The apartment status of such apartement will be changed from "Pending" to "Available"
  3. The place is fake: You can click on this button if the the picture uploaded on DotLive doesn`t match the real image of the apartment seen. On click of this button, the amount of the apartment paid is moved from your transaction wallet to your main wallet. You can either withdraw the money available in your main wallet or use it to get another apartment. The apartment status of such apartement will be changed from "Pending" to "Disqualified". The agent/landlord who uploaded such apartment shall be disqualified from uploading another apartment for a period of 30days.

We strongly advise that when you are going to visit and verify your apartment, that you go with more one or two persons in order to avoid unplanned incidents as DotLive shall not be responsible for any lack of safety precaution not taken by the user.
